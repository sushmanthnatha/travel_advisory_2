High security risk (level 4) in connection with the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19) and consequent severe restrictions in travel. It is strongly advised to postpone non-essential travel and to make use of existing return options. Travelers from COVID-19 high-risk countries (including Austria) is no longer permitted to enter.
Congolese nationals and foreigners with valid residence permit are subject when entering from high-risk countries, a 14-day quarantine.
Traveling in the affected areas are advised to avoid large gatherings of people to follow the instructions of local security authorities sequence and strictly comply with the hygienic precautions. General travel warnings concerning COVID-19 can be found on the homepage of the Austrian Foreign Ministry. Additional information on COVID-19 can be found on the websites of the Ministry of Social Affairs, Health, Care and Consumer Protection and the Weltgesundheitsorganisation.In the provinces of North Kivu and Ituri - for which in any case partial travel warnings exist - cases of Ebola virus have been detected.
