High security risk (level 4) in connection with the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19) and consequent severe restrictions in travel. It is strongly advised to postpone non-essential travel and to make use of existing return options. Due to the possible spread of the coronavirus (SARS-CoV-2) (international Djibouti Airport, Central Station Nagad, border crossings Loyada, Glail, Galafi and Balho and ports Tdjourah and Obock) a paid PCR Covid test is in the context of immigration formalities done , The test result remains to be seen.
Must be emigrants (open daily from 8:00 to 12:00) at the health center Bouffard before departure to make a paid Covid test and wait for the result, so sufficient time should be properly taken into account.
Tested positive entry and exit willing to be isolated either private or clinically depending on the disease. For foreigners without a residence permit, the insulation is chargeable.
All hygienic measures (mask duty, spacing, hand hygiene, ...) must be respected.
More information about the coronavirus can be found on the website of the Ministry of Social Affairs, Health, Care and Consumer Protection and the World Health Organization.
