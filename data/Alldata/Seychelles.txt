High security risk (level 4) in connection with the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19). With continuing restrictions in the flight and travel as well as far-reaching restrictions in public life can be expected until further notice. Of non-essential travel is not advised. Flight connections are currently set. The land borders were closed on March 27 for passenger transport. On March 24, the state of emergency was declared and banned mass gatherings. It is a nightly curfew between 23:00 and 05:00. Travel between district boundaries are again allowed. In public spaces social distancing provisions and a mask duty apply. In general, expected to continue limitations of public life. Travelers are advised to avoid large gatherings of people to follow the instructions of local security authorities sequence and strictly comply with the hygienic precautions.
