High security risk (level 4) in connection with the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19) and consequent severe restrictions in travel. It is strongly advised to postpone non-essential travel and to make use of existing return options. Due to the possible spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19) all Congolese border crossings were closed, including airports for passenger traffic.
More information about the coronavirus can be found on the website of the Ministry of Social Affairs, Health, Care and Consumer Protection and the World Health Organization.
