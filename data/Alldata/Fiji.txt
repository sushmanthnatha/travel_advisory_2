High security risk (level 4) in connection with the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19) and consequent severe restrictions in travel. It is strongly advised to postpone non-essential travel and to make use of existing return options.
It is a travel ban on all foreign travelers who were within 14 days prior to entry to Fiji in China (mainland), Italy, Iran, Spain, South Korea, the UK and all European countries. All persons entering to Fiji, must undergo a 14-day self-isolation.
Also, find at all airports (Singapore, Hong Kong, Tokyo) from which flights to Fiji go Chill checks ( "thermal screenings") as well as health checks on arrival instead.
