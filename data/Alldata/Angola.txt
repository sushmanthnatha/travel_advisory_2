High security risk (level 4) in connection with the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19). With continuing restrictions in the flight and travel as well as far-reaching restrictions in public life can be expected until further notice. Of non-essential travel is not advised. All international flights to and from Angola are set until further notice since March 20, 2020, and the national air traffic is suspended. Land and sea borders are also closed during this period for passenger traffic. The since March 27 to June 24 proclaimed a state of emergency extended in a weakened form. A further extension is possible. During the state of emergency, it is forbidden to cross the national borders.
