To curb coronavirus "6 golden rules of conduct" apply in Belgium, compliance with hygiene regulations, such as regular hand washing or disinfection, handshakes and hugs avoided. Activities preferably outdoors, if not possible, ventilate the area sufficiently. Special consideration for people who belong to a risk group. 1.5 m minimum distance, except for children under 12, people's own household and personal contact circle. Personal contact circuit ( "Bubble"): Meeting allows a maximum of 15 people per week outside the home. All group meetings (whether at home or outside the home) are limited to a maximum of 15 people, including children.
